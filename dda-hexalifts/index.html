about us

Hexa lifts is a subsidiary company owned by Ventac Elevators. Established in 2009, Hexa Lifts comprises of an integrated team of Engineers and Technicians with 10-20 years experience in various aspects of the Elevator INDUSTRY namely Design, Estimation, Execution, Operation and After sales service and Maintenance.

We have installed above 700 lifts with 100% customer satisfaction.
